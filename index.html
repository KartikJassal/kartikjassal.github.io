A /Who I Am

About Me

I am pursuing a Specialized Honours Bachelor of Engineering in Space Engineering at York University, with an expected graduation date of April 2029. My work sits at the intersection of robotics, systems engineering, and human spaceflight.

I am especially interested in how on-orbit servicing can make space operations more sustainable, resilient, and scalable. Through research, prototyping, and student leadership, I am building the technical judgement and communication skills needed to contribute to multidisciplinary space missions.

Outside engineering, I enjoy rock climbing and logic puzzles. Both reinforce the patience, deliberate practice, and problem-solving mindset I bring to technical work.
